Dom Scola Portfolio

Hello, my name is Dom Scola and welcome to my online resume and portfolio. I am a graduate student at the University of Michigan School of Information. I am studying Human-Computer Interaction and I will graduate in May 2017. I am interested is usability and UX design and I am building my skills in programming as well as data science and data analytics. This summer, I will be working for Ford Motor Company in Dearborn, Michigan as part of the Global Data Insights and Analytics department.
